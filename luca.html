The name Luca

Story and style

Luca is a traditional and short name for boys. It is usually linked with from Lucania, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It has Latin roots and has been used as a given name in European naming traditions.

As a baby name, Luca feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Lucas, Luka, Luke. Because Luca is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Lu can work casually.
