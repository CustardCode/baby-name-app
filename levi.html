The name Levi

Story and style

Levi is a biblical and historic name for boys. It is usually linked with joined or attached, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious.

For parents, Levi is easy to imagine in everyday life: simple enough for a child, but still polished enough to grow with them. It also has a Biblical background, which may appeal to families who like names with Jewish or Christian tradition. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Levy. Because Levi is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Lev can work casually.
