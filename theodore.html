The name Theodore

Story and style

Meaning

Theodore is most commonly linked to ideas of gift of God. That meaning gives the name a clear little story without making it feel overworked.

Origin

Theodore is commonly traced to Greek given name. It is associated with Greek roots. It is connected with the root name Theodoros.

History and background

Theodore has older roots than many modern baby names, which helps explain why it can feel established rather than trend-made.

What style of name is Theodore?

Theodore has a historic, established feel without sounding overly formal. It sits naturally beside names like Leo, Henry, Arthur and Felix: names that feel related in sound, style or popularity.
