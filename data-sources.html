Coverage notes

What the data means

Rankings are based on available Australian baby-name data. If a name is missing from a year, it may mean the name was outside the published list, not that it had zero births.

Years covered2008-2025, with some additional state and reported national rows where available.
Gender coverageBoys and girls.
List typeMostly published top-100 rankings. Counts are only shown where the source provides them.
Meaning and origin metadataStored separately in data/clean/name_metadata.csv. Not every name has meaning data yet, and meanings or origins can vary by source.
