The name Jasper

Story and style

Jasper is a historic and traditional name for boys. It is usually linked with treasurer, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It has Latin roots and has been used as a given name in European naming traditions.

As a baby name, Jasper feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It also has a Christian tradition link through the traditional names of the Magi, although the Bible itself does not give the Magi names. Related forms include Casper, Gaspar, Kasper. Nickname options include Jas, Jazz.
