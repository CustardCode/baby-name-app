The name Leonardo

Story and style

Leonardo is a historic and traditional name for boys. It is usually linked to the lion, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It has Latin roots and has been used as a given name in European naming traditions.

As a baby name, Leonardo feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. Related forms include Leon, Leonardo, Leopold. It is often shortened to Lee.
