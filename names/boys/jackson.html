The name Jackson

Story and style

Jackson is a traditional and biblical name for boys. It is usually linked with God is gracious, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It is connected to the Hebrew name John.

For parents, Jackson is easy to imagine in everyday life: simple enough for a child, but still polished enough to grow with them. Related forms include Jackson, Jacques. It is often shortened to Jackie.
