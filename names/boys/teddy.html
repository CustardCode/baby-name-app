The name Teddy

Story and style

Teddy is a historic and traditional name for boys. It is usually linked to ideas of gift of God, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It is connected to the older root name Theodoros.

As a baby name, Teddy feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. Related forms include Theo, Theodor, Teodor. Nickname options include Theo, Teddy.
