The name Theo

Story and style

Theo is a historic and traditional name for boys. It is usually linked to ideas of gift of God, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It is connected to the older root name Theodoros.

As a baby name, Theo feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Theo, Theodor, Teodor. Because Theo is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Theo can work casually.
