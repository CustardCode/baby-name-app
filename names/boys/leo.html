The name Leo

Story and style

Leo is a historic and traditional name for boys. It comes directly from the Latin word leo, meaning lion. That gives the name a strong, simple image without making it feel heavy or old-fashioned. It has Latin roots and has been used as a given name in European naming traditions.

As a baby name, Leo feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Leon, Leonardo, Leopold. Because Leo is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Lee can work casually.
