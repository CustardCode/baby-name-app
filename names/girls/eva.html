The name Eva

Story and style

Eva is a short name for girls. It is connected to the older root name Ava.

As a baby name, Eva feels compact, easy to say and at home in more than one language. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Eva, Eve. Because Eva is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Avie can work casually.
