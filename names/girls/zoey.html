The name Zoey

Story and style

Zoey is a historic and traditional name for girls. It is usually linked to life, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It is connected to the older root name Zoe.

As a baby name, Zoey feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Zoey, Zoie. Because Zoey is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Zo can work casually.
