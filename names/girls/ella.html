The name Ella

Story and style

Ella is a short name for girls.

As a baby name, Ella feels compact, easy to say and at home in more than one language. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Elle, Ela. Because Ella is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Ellie can work casually.
