The name Hannah

Story and style

Hannah is a biblical and historic name for girls. It is usually linked to ideas of favor or grace, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It is connected to the Hebrew name Channah.

For parents, Hannah is easy to imagine in everyday life: simple enough for a child, but still polished enough to grow with them. It also has a Biblical background, which may appeal to families who like names with Jewish or Christian tradition. Related forms include Hanna, Hana. Nickname options include Han, Annie.
