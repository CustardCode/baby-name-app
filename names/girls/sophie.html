Background notes

Meaning

Sophie is most commonly linked to wisdom. That meaning gives the name a clear little story without making it feel overworked.

Origin

Sophie is commonly traced to French form of Sophia. It is associated with Greek or French roots. It is connected with the root name Sophia.

History and background

Sophie has older roots than many modern baby names, which helps explain why it can feel established rather than trend-made.

What style of name is Sophie?

Sophie feels traditional, familiar and steady. It sits naturally beside names like Chloe, Emily, Grace and Clara: names that feel related in sound, style or popularity.
