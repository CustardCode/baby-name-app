The name Isabel

Story and style

Isabel is a biblical and historic name for girls. It is usually linked with my God is an oath, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It has Latin roots and has been used as a given name in European naming traditions.

As a baby name, Isabel feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It also has a Biblical background, which may appeal to families who like names with Jewish or Christian tradition. Related forms include Elisabeth, Eliza, Isabel. Nickname options include Liz, Lizzie, Beth.
