The name Abby

Story and style

Abby is a biblical and historic name for girls. It is usually linked to ideas of my father is joy, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. It is connected to the Hebrew name Abigail.

For parents, Abby is easy to imagine in everyday life: simple enough for a child, but still polished enough to grow with them. It also has a Biblical background, which may appeal to families who like names with Jewish or Christian tradition. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Abigayle, Abigale. Because Abby is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Abby can work casually.
