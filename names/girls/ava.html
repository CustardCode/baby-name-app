The name Ava

Story and style

Ava is a short name for girls.

As a baby name, Ava feels compact, easy to say and at home in more than one language. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Eva, Eve. Because Ava is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Avie can work casually.
