The name Hazel

Story and style

Hazel is a nature-inspired and vintage name for girls. It is usually linked to the hazel tree, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. The word can also describe the warm brown-green colour associated with hazel eyes, giving the name a natural and earthy feel. Hazel is best understood as an English nature name rather than a name built from ancient personal-name elements.

As a baby name, Hazel feels natural, gentle and a little vintage. It is not usually treated as a Biblical, Catholic or traditional saint name. Related forms include Hazell. Nickname options include Haze, Hazy.
