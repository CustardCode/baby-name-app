The name Gracie

Story and style

Gracie is a classic and gentle name for girls. It is usually linked to ideas of grace, favour or blessing, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. Its Latin connection gives it a wider meaning family around favour, thanks, kindness and divine grace. It belongs to the English virtue-name tradition, alongside names such as Faith, Hope, Mercy and Patience.

As a baby name, Gracie feels simple, classic and timeless. Related forms include Gracia, Grazia. It is often shortened to Gracie.
