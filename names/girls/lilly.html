The name Lilly

Story and style

Lilly is a nature-inspired and vintage name for girls. It is usually linked to the lily flower, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. Lilly is best understood as an English nature name rather than a name built from ancient personal-name elements.

As a baby name, Lilly feels natural, gentle and a little vintage. It is not usually treated as a Biblical, Catholic or traditional saint name. Related forms include Lilly, Lillie, Lilian. Nickname options include Lil, Lils.
