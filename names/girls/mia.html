The name Mia

Story and style

Mia is a short and bright name for girls. It is usually described as a short form of Maria and other names, so it has links to a broader name family while still feeling complete on its own. It is connected to the older root name Maria.

As a baby name, Mia feels compact, easy to say and at home in more than one language. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Miah, Miya. Because Mia is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Mimi can work casually.
