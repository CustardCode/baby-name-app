The name Olivia

Story and style

Olivia is a nature-inspired and vintage name for girls. It is usually linked to the olive tree or olive-associated, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. Olivia is best understood as an English nature name rather than a name built from ancient personal-name elements.

As a baby name, Olivia feels natural, gentle and a little vintage. Related forms include Olivie. Nickname options include Liv, Livvy.
