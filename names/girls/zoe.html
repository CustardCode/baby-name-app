The name Zoe

Story and style

Zoe is a historic and traditional name for girls. It is usually linked to life, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious.

As a baby name, Zoe feels established and familiar without sounding too formal. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Zoey, Zoie. Because Zoe is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Zo can work casually.
