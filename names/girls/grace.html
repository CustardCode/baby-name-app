The name Grace

Story and style

Grace is a classic and gentle name for girls. It is usually linked to ideas of grace, favour or blessing, giving it a clear meaning without making the name feel too serious. Its Latin connection gives it a wider meaning family around favour, thanks, kindness and divine grace. It belongs to the English virtue-name tradition, alongside names such as Faith, Hope, Mercy and Patience.

As a baby name, Grace feels simple, classic and timeless. It has strong Christian associations because grace is an important idea in Christian theology, but it is also used more broadly to suggest elegance, kindness and goodwill. Related forms include Gracia, Grazia. It is often shortened to Gracie.
