The name Eve

Story and style

Eve is a short name for girls. It is connected to the older root name Ava.

As a baby name, Eve feels compact, easy to say and at home in more than one language. It can stand on its own or sit beside longer related forms such as Eva, Eve. Because Eve is already short, it does not really need a nickname, though Avie can work casually.
